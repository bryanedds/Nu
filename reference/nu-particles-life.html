Life Type

Namespace: Nu.Particles
Assembly: Nu.dll
Base Type: ValueType
All Interfaces: IEquatable<Life> , IStructuralEquatable , IComparable<Life> , IComparable , IStructuralComparable
Kind: Struct

Describes the life of an instance value. OPTIMIZATION: LifeTimeOpt uses GameTime.zero to represent infinite life. OPTIMIZATION: doesn't use Liveness type to avoid its constructor calls. OPTIMIZATION: pre-computes progress scalar to minimize number of divides.
